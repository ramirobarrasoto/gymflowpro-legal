Privacy Policy

GymFlow Pro
Last Updated: January 2, 2026

GymFlow Pro ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal information and your rights regarding that data.

1. Introduction

Developed by Ramiro Barra Soto in Melbourne, Australia, GymFlow Pro provides workout tracking and analysis while ensuring the security of your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Details: Email address and full name for account synchronization.
  • Workout Data: Exercise history, sets, reps, weights, and training notes.
  • Media Files: User-uploaded photos and videos for exercise documentation.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Precise GPS location (except when explicitly authorized for gym-finder features).
  • Biometric or external health app data.

3. Virtual Assistance & AI

We utilize proprietary algorithms and artificial intelligence ("Virtual Assistants") to analyze training patterns. This processing provides personalized coaching insights while maintaining your personal identity protected at all times.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS during transmission. We implement industry-standard security protocols to ensure your training data remains private.
